About

Our beautiful studio is fully equipped for yoga and offers a range of classes suitable for absolute beginners through to experienced practitioners. We teach traditional yoga in a safe, supportive and non-competitive environment with a strong emphasis on learning through practice.

Meet our Team

Tessa Meek

Tessa has been practising Iyengar yoga for 27 years. In 1998 she started teaching classes at the Aro Valley Community Hall and has been teaching ever since. At the start of 2007, Tessa opened the Iyengar Yoga Centre of Wellington.

Jo Peden

Jo started Iyengar yoga in 1996 and was quickly captivated by the practice of yoga, finding it both challenging and inspiring. She commenced teaching in 2001. Jo teaches the Tuesday and Sunday 10am classes and she also teaches the monthly Sunday Led Class and workshops.

Agata Orlowicz

Agata was first introduced to yoga in 2000 at one of Poland’s oldest Iyengar yoga studios. However, it wasn’t until 2006 that she fully committed and started to practice daily. Agata teaches the Wednesday 7.15pm Level 1 class.

Gina Inverarity

Gina started Iyengar yoga in 2004 when she was expecting her first daughter. She found yoga immensely beneficial during pregnancy and after that a welcome respite when her children were young. Gina teaches the Beginner Courses, Monday and Wednesday 10am classes, and the Level 1 on Sunday at 11.45am as well as regularly filling in for other teachers.

Penny Mitropopulos

Penny started her first journey into Iyengar yoga in 1997 under the teaching of Louisa Potter, at that time in Nelson at the Nelson Yoga Centre. After a few years she traveled to India to develop her practice and understanding. Penny teaches the early morning Friday class.

Rosie Percival

Rosie began practicing Iyengar yoga in 2005 as a way to maintain general health and learn to touch her toes. After traveling and experimenting with other styles of yoga, she was drawn back to the discipline and methodology of Iyengar yoga. Rosie regularly fills in for other teachers.
